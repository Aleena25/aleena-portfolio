I'm a Backend Engineer with 5+ years of hands-on experience architecting and delivering production-grade, scalable backend systems for enterprise clients across the GCC region, currently based in Abu Dhabi, UAE, and now focused on AI/LLM Engineering.

My foundation is Python, Django, and FastAPI — distributed systems design, event-driven Kafka pipelines, RBAC security, and PostgreSQL performance engineering for real enterprise workloads. I'm now applying that same production discipline to LLM applications: building RAG pipelines, integrating LLM APIs (Groq, Llama, OpenAI-compatible endpoints), and working with LangChain, LangGraph, and vector databases to ship AI features that hold up in production — not just in notebooks.

I also run an educational YouTube channel, SkillsBytebyAleena, where I break down LLM, RAG, and agent concepts for other Python developers — a habit that keeps my own understanding sharp and forces me to explain systems clearly, not just use them.

Before backend engineering, I led ML and radar-based AI R&D in the Oil & Gas sector. I hold an M.Tech in Signal Processing and have always worked at the intersection of applied ML and production systems.
